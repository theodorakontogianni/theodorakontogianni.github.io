Theodora Kontogianni

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the ETH AI Center with Prof. Dr. Konrad Schindler and Prof. Dr. Siyu Tang.
Prior to this I received my PhD at the Computer Vision Group with Prof. Dr. Bastian Leibe at RWTH Aachen University.
My research interest are computer vision, 3D scene understanding, interactive and continual learning.
