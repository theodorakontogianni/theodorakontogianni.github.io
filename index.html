Theodora Kontogianni

I am an Assistant Professor in 3D Computer Vision at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at the Technical University of Denmark . Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the ETH AI Center with Prof. Dr. Konrad Schindler and Prof. Dr. Siyu Tang .

Before this, I received my PhD from the Computer Vision Group under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Bastian Leibe at RWTH Aachen University . I have also done two internships at Google Research, one in the US and one in Zurich.

My research interests are in computer vision, with a focus on 3D scene understanding, interactive learning, and continual learning.
